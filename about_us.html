HISTORY

Copenhagen Fashion Week is the leading Nordic fashion event, uniting creativity, responsibility, and innovation. Twice a year, the city becomes a global stage for established and emerging designers who define the future of fashion through craftsmanship, vision, and conscious design.

Rooted in Scandinavian values, CPHFW sets the standard for sustainability in the fashion industry. The event's Sustainability Requirements ensure that every participating brand is committed to reducing impact and shaping a more responsible future for fashion.

  • SUSTAINIBILITY

Copenhagen Fashion Week aims to establish Copenhagen as a leading fashion week with sustainability as its core focus. We want to inspire and encourage the fashion industry to take responsible action by setting forward-thinking standards, supporting innovative brands, and driving positive change.

MISSION

To redefine the future of fashion by positioning Copenhagen as a global leader in responsible, creative, and innovative fashion - where sustainability and style go hand in hand.

SUSTAINIBILITY

CPHFW showcases collections that are innovative, stylish, and responsibly made, proving fashion can be both creative and conscious.

"Sustainability is not an option, it's at the heart of everything we do at Copenhagen Fashion Week."

At Copenhagen Fashion Week, sustainability means every participating brand meets strict standards for materials, production, and ethical practices.
